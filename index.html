About Autocon


SECURE

Autocon is 100% secure. No bloatware, no adds, no-nonsense! You can also use it as a way to protect your Desktop

FAST

Autocon, made in C++, is blazingly fast! It uses the entire power of your system to simulate clicks and automate tasks.

Reliable

Autocon has a simple and visually appealing interface! And performs consistently under various conditions and workloads!
